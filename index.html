Research Abstract

This research proposes a comprehensive defense-in-depth framework to mitigate Ransomware-as-a-Service (RaaS) threats in hybrid cloud environments. By integrating OWASP ZAP (vulnerability prevention), Snort (real-time detection), and GnuPG (data encryption), our strategy addresses the complete attack lifecycle while aligning with NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 principles.
